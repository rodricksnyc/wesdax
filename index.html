Features
Analyze data quickly and easily generates statistics (frequencies, percentiles, means), tables and figures that can be cited in studies, presentations, reports, or briefings
Weighted and unweighted data can be displayed in frequency distributions, cross-tabs, means, percentiles, confidence intervals, and statistical testing
Custom reports written in SAS® can be parameterized to enable users to optimize/tailor their output with seamless integration. These results can be displayed in various formats from Excel to PDF
Robust confidentiality treatments are available — even though case-level data are not displayed, disclosure risks may remain if non-public data are used
WesVar® is the analytic engine for WesDaX, developed by Westat , and
analyzes data from simple to the most complex sample designs
Out of the box design allows for quick set up with minimal
labor for projects and datasets of any size

Research Data Sets

Utilize public use data sets to gain valuable insights into the results of large national surveys and census. Analyze huge amounts of data to discover trends and other useful visualizations.

Select a data set to explore:
American Community Survey
The American Community Survey (ACS) is the largest survey in the US, conducted by the US Census Bureau , and covers topics related to disability, educational attainment, employment, journey to work, income, language proficiency, migration, and housing characteristics.
Current Population Survey
The Current Population Survey (CPS) Annual Social and Economic Supplement (ASEC) is a large national survey, conducted jointly by the US Bureau of Labor Statistics  and US Census Bureau , that provides data generally relating to income, poverty, and health insurance coverage.
Common Core of Data
The Common Core of Data (CCD) provides comprehensive data (e.g., enrollment by grade) on a national census of public
elementary and secondary schools and school districts sponsored by the US Department of Education .
